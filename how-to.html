How to use User Journals?

User Journals are designed to enhance User Stories with 6 familiar key questions: who, what, when, where, why and how.

Enhance

Use UJ's alongside standard User Stories to enhance them. UJ's help to make sure that the most fundamental aspects of the user's needs are capture in a succinct fashion.
